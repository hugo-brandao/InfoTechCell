Is it okay to let the phone completely discharge?

It is not advisable to let the battery fully discharge, "as in some cases the battery cannot start a new charge cycle and in more extreme cases it needs to be replaced".

Is it okay to take out your cell phone before 100?

The batteries currently used in electronic equipment are those made of lithium ion polymer, which do not require complete cycles of charging and discharging. Therefore, the user can disconnect the equipment from the outlet before the battery is 100% charged.
